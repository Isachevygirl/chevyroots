# ChevyRoots News Sources

Master list of sources to check regularly for Chevy news. Organized by tier.

## Tier 1 — Check Daily (Core Chevy Sources)
- **GM Authority** (gmauthority.com) — Best dedicated GM/Chevy news site. Covers every model, recall, deal, and announcement
- **Corvette Blogger** (corvetteblogger.com) — Everything Corvette: news, auctions, racing, production updates
- **GM News / Chevrolet Newsroom** (news.gm.com / media.chevrolet.com) — Official press releases, product announcements
- **Chevy Hardcore** (chevyhardcore.com) — Enthusiast-focused builds, performance, culture
- **GM-Trucks.com** — Silverado, Colorado, Tahoe/Suburban truck-focused community and news

- **NASCAR.com** (nascar.com) — Cup Series results, Chevy team news
- **RACER** (racer.com) — IndyCar, IMSA, WEC coverage
- **Motorsport.com** (motorsport.com) — Global racing coverage

## Tier 5 — Check Weekly (Culture, Auctions, Classics)
- **Hagerty** (hagerty.com/media) — Market trends, classic values, barn finds
- **Bring a Trailer** (bringatrailer.com) — Auction results, notable sales
- **Barrett-Jackson** (barrett-jackson.com) — Major auction results

- **LSX Magazine** (lsxmag.com) — LS/LT engine builds and swaps
- **All Chevy Performance** (allchevyperformance.com) — Chevy-specific builds and events
- **Goodguys** (good-guys.com) — Car show events and coverage
- **SEMA** (sema.org) — SEMA Show builds and awards
- **Petersen Automotive Museum** (petersen.org) — Exhibits, culture
- **Restomods** (restomods.com) — Restomod builds and culture
