Official Vehicle of MLB

Chevy at the Ballpark

Chevrolet has been the Official Vehicle of Major League Baseball for over 20 years — a partnership as American as the game itself. From spring training to the Fall Classic, Chevy trucks and SUVs are on the field, in the lots, and behind the scenes at every ballpark in the country.

In 2026, with America's 250th anniversary aligning with a landmark baseball season, the connection between Chevrolet and the national pastime has never felt more fitting. Look for Chevy activations at every MLB stadium this season.
